What’s Really Happening During Conflict?

Most relationship conflicts aren't about the surface issue. They are driven by emotional activation — often misunderstood as anger, frustration, or defensiveness.

When these emotions are suppressed, they create distance.

When they explode, they cause lasting damage.

By understanding these emotions consciously, they can become a pathway back to clarity, connection, and trust.
