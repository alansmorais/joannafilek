How can I help you?

I work mainly with people experiencing emotional crises, difficulties in daily functioning, and relationship challenges.

Crises and emotional difficulties

Depression, anxiety, chronic stress, burnout, grief, life crises.

Relationships and intimacy

Communication difficulties, relationship crises, feeling lonely, building healthy relationships.

Self-esteem

Low self-esteem, difficulty setting boundaries, inner critic, self-acceptance.

Sexual difficulties

Premature ejaculation, lack of sexual desire, difficulties with erection and orgasm.

Pain and discomfort

Dyspareunia, vaginismus, pain during intercourse, and psychogenic physical blockages.

Sexual anxiety and shame

Fear of body judgment, lowered self-worth, support in accepting one's sexuality.

What does the first consultation look like?

The first meeting is a calm conversation about your situation, difficulties, and needs.

1.

We talk without pressure

You can say as much as you feel ready to. You don't have to force anything.

2.

We establish needs

Together we determine what you want to work on and what support you need.

3.

We create a safe space

We work in complete discretion, without judgment, shaming, or time pressure.

4.

We look for a solution

Together we develop a therapy plan that will help you regain balance.
