How can I help you?

I primarily work with individuals experiencing emotional crises, difficulties in daily functioning, and relationship challenges.

Crises and emotional difficulties

Depression, anxiety, chronic stress, burnout, grief, life crises.

Relationships and intimacy

Communication difficulties, relationship crises, feeling lonely, building healthy relationships.

Self-esteem

Low self-esteem, difficulty setting boundaries, inner critic, self-acceptance.

Personal development

Better understanding of oneself, emotional regulation, building mental resilience.

Sexual difficulties and intimacy

Sexual problems with an emotional background (anxiety, shame, blockages).

Support during changes

Life transitions, decisions, searching for meaning and direction.

Psychotherapy and Sexological Consultations – FAQ

What does professional private psychotherapy look like?

A private psychotherapy clinic offers safe and fully confidential support for people struggling with life crises, anxiety, or burnout. If you are looking for a proven psychotherapist, a professional psychological consultation helps identify the source of problems and implement effective strategies for coping with daily difficulties.

When is it worth seeking a sexological consultation?

Problems with intimacy and communication in a relationship affect many people. If you are looking for a specialist who will help overcome shame and reduce tension, a sexological consultation may be the right step. A specialist's support helps couples and individuals regain harmony and satisfaction in their intimate lives.

Is couples therapy a good solution for a relationship in crisis?

Yes, couples therapy helps rebuild strained bonds and improve mutual communication. Sessions in the clinic allow for identifying destructive conflict patterns and developing healthier ways of communicating, regardless of the length and nature of the relationship.
