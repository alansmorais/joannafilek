Global Support • Expat Therapy

Online Therapy for Expats.
Support anywhere you are.

Living abroad brings unique challenges. Whether you are navigating cultural differences in Norway, the US, or adjusting to life in Poland, I provide a safe, English-speaking space to work through stress, anxiety, relationship issues, and emotional crises.

Schedule an Online Call

Why Online Therapy?

Online psychotherapy is highly effective and offers the flexibility you need when living an international lifestyle.

Therapy in Your Language

Expressing complex emotions in a second language can be difficult. I offer therapy in fluent English and Polish, ensuring nothing gets lost in translation.

Overcoming Expat Isolation

Moving to countries like Norway or the US can trigger feelings of loneliness, cultural shock, or identity crises. We can work through these unique expat challenges together.

Flexible Scheduling

Different time zones shouldn't prevent you from getting help. Online booking allows you to find slots that work for your schedule, directly from your home.

International Relationships

Cross-cultural couples often face unique communication hurdles. I provide online sexological counseling and couples therapy tailored to international dynamics.

Distance shouldn't stop you from healing.

Take the first step toward emotional balance from the comfort of your own home, wherever that may be today.
